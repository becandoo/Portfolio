About

When I was a kid, I would get in trouble for taking things apart, just to learn how they worked. This worked out in my favor when I asked for a computer for my 13th birthday. I was met with a stern "No", but pleasantly surprised when a computer was waiting for when I arrived home on that awesome day. This started my addiction to technology and I began taking as many technology related courses from middle school into high school.

When the time came to go to college and choose a major, the field was obvious, but what segment of technology did I like best? Inititially I explored software development, teaching myself C# to start.

Eventually, I was asked to build a website for a friend and began exploring the world of web development and realized this was where I wanted to be. At the time, I was working a technical lead for a local IT Services company and after noticing my enthusiasm for ASP.NET Development, I was lucky enough to be involved in some SharePoint development projects.

I began approacing small businesses and non-profits, offering to build free websites to help build my portfolio, and quickly began building skills while helping my local community.

I am now working as an ASP.NET Web Developer for an E-Commerce company in Portland, OR.
